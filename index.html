About Me


My name is Donnie Toombs and I am from Charleston, SC. I currently hold a Bachelors in Finance and an MBA. Upon obtaining my MBA and working in the Finance/Investment industries for a few years, I decided I wanted to pursue further education in another field I have a passion for. I decided to immerse myself in web/software development by attending and completing an intensive Full Stack Web/Software Development program covering multiple languages and technologies across the full stack. When I am not reading about the capital markets or coding, I do enjoy golfing and working in my wood shop.

I bring a unique skillset that has been obtained by pursuing multiple degrees and by working and gaining experience in multiple industries. I have past experience in the hospitality/restaurant and banking/finance/investment industries where I have gained skills including management/leadership, customer service, sales, analysis, presentations, branding, marketing, data analysis, strategy development and execution just to name a few. Recently, I have obtained more technology skills with experience in multiple programming languages and frameworks in a full stack curriculum. My past experience lends itself to being extremely flexible and able to adapt to change.

While I have worked in many areas wearing multiple hats, the true passion I possess is the love of learning and improvement! I will forever thirst for knowledge and that is what would make me a great addition to your team, company, or project. The technology field is one that is everchanging so it is fitting that someone like myself is a part of it.
